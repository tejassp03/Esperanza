About Us

DSATM with its heritage has fortified numerous students lives to peaks of success within their 4 years of graduate life. We are proud that the DSATM family has been awarded NAAC A+ Accreditation. Art and culture play an important role in the development of DSI. It represents a set of shared attitudes, values, goals and practices.

Each year DSATM comes with its annual extravagant inter college techno-cultural fest where the family of DSATM gets to showcase their amazing talent! Esperanza is packed up with spellbinding cultural events, amusing activities, fun filled games, line up of stellar visual artists and what not. Come and immerse yourself in the vibe and get immersed in the fun and frolic. We are all really excited for you to grace us with your presence on the 24th and 25th of June, 2022.
