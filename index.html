About Us

DSATM Campus

 Dayananda Sagar Academy of Technology and Management with its heritage has fortified numerous students lives to peaks of success within their 4 years of graduate life. It has a scintillating atmosphere with lush greenery, bringing inspiration and new experience to learn. Culture, Art & Performances, sports & fitness blend with high academic education brings out the inner potential of the student to the maximum.

Culture plays an important role in the development of DSI. It represents a set of shared attitudes, values, goals and practices. Culture and creativity manifest themselves in almost all economic, social and other activities.

Each year DSATM comes with its annual extravagant inter college techno-cultural fest- Esperanza, where the family of DSATM gets to showcase their amazing talent! Esperanza is packed up with spellbinding cultural events, amusing activities, fun filled games, line up of stellar visual artists and what not. This is not just it; at Esperanza, the college will be announcing the champions of the inter-department competitions conducted by the cultural club of DSATM, Antaragni. The winning department will be rewarded with an exquisite trophy. Immerse yourself in the vibe and get immersed in the fun and frolic. We are all really excited for you to grace us with your presence on the 24th and 25th of June, 2022.
